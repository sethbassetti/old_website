My Story

Hi! I'm Seth Bassetti, a machine learning engineer and researcher from Cle Elum, WA. I have a passion for building machine learning models and exploring how we can push the limits on artifical intelligence technologies!

I discovered computer science through pure chance while living a nomadic van life in the southwestern US. On a semester off of my undergraduate, I met someone on the road who introduced me to coding, which convinced me to continue my studies in a new field!

Since then, I have worked as a python developer in fintech, an ducator and product manager with AI Camp, and a graduate researcher in computer vision at Western Washingon University! I continually find myself excited by new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what is possible with artifial intelligence.
